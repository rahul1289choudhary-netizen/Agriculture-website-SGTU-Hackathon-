Understanding Our Platform

How it works, the problem it solves, and how to get started.

1

Enter Your Location & Farm Details

Tell us your state, district, season, farm size, and irrigation. This loads region-specific soil baselines and cropping patterns for your area.

2

AI Predicts Your Soil Values

No soil test needed. Our AI automatically predicts N, P, K, pH and moisture from regional soil databases, satellite data, and historical records.

3

Set Your Crop Preferences

Choose your goal — maximize yield, profit, or sustainability. Select your preferred crop type and fertilizer budget for tailored AI recommendations.

4

Get Full AI-Powered Analysis

The AI analyses your data against regional crop databases, yield records, and market prices. You get: best crop, fertilizer plan, predicted yield, and ecological alerts.

5

Use Feature Tools Anytime

Click any feature card on the homepage for instant AI tools — Yield Prediction, Fertilizer Guidance, Market Prices, and Ecological Risks — just pick state & crop.

💡 Live Card: The Soil Analysis card on the homepage updates in real-time as you fill in your location in the Analyze page. Watch your soil data appear instantly!

Over / Under Application of Fertilizers

Farmers apply fertilizers without knowing what nutrients their soil needs — leading to waste, soil degradation, and reduced profits.

📉

Significant Yield Gaps Across India

Major yield gaps of 1.7–4.6 t/ha exist between actual and attainable yields across Andhra Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Punjab and Uttar Pradesh.

💸

Increased Farming Costs

Guesswork-based fertilization raises input costs without improving output, directly reducing farmer income and profitability each season.

🌍

Environmental Degradation

Excess fertilizer runoff pollutes water systems, reduces biodiversity, and causes long-term damage to soil quality and structure.

🐦

Ecological Risks Ignored

Bird density, pest outbreak patterns, and monoculture risks are often overlooked in traditional farming advisories given to farmers.
